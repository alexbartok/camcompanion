CamCompanion Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

CamCompanion does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. We have no servers. Everything the app does happens on your device and on your own dashcam.

What the app accesses, and why

  • Local network access. The app communicates with your dashcam over the Wi-Fi network that the dashcam itself broadcasts. This connection is used solely to browse, stream, download, and configure recordings on hardware you own. No data is sent to us or to any third party over this connection.
  • Photo library (add only). When you choose to save a recording or photo, the app adds that file to your device's photo library. The app does not read, browse, or upload your existing photos.
  • On-device storage. Recordings you download are stored on your device and remain under your control. You can delete them at any time.

What the app does NOT do

  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising.
  • No user accounts, sign-in, or profiles.
  • No collection of contacts, browsing history, or usage data.
  • No transmission of your recordings, location, or any personal data to us or to any third party.

Optional diagnostics

The app includes an optional Diagnostics feature that is turned off by default. If you turn it on and then choose to send a diagnostic report to the developer (always by your explicit action, via the system share sheet):

  • The report may contain technical information about how the app communicated with your dashcam (e.g. request/response data used to fix compatibility problems).
  • Wi-Fi passwords are removed from diagnostic reports before they can be shared.
  • Location data is excluded by default. It is only included if you explicitly choose to include it.
  • Reports are used solely to diagnose and fix problems, and are deleted after analysis.

Nothing is ever sent automatically. If you never enable Diagnostics and never tap "share," no diagnostic data ever leaves your device.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
