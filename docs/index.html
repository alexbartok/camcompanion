View and save footage from your Wi-Fi dashcam, from your iPhone.

What CamCompanion does

  • Connects to your dashcam over its own Wi-Fi network.
  • Live view, browse driving / parking / event recordings and photos.
  • Download clips and photos to your iPhone's Photos library.
  • Adjust recording, overlay, and system settings on the camera.

Frequently asked questions

How do I connect to my dashcam?
Your dashcam broadcasts its own Wi-Fi network. On your iPhone, join that network in Settings → Wi-Fi, then open CamCompanion.
I don't have the camera with me — can I still try the app?
Yes. Tap Try Demo Mode on the Home screen (or turn on the Demo Mode toggle in Settings → Diagnostics) to explore the app with built-in sample data.
The recording dates look wrong.
Recordings are stamped with the camera's own clock at the time of recording. If the camera lost power, its clock can reset until it reacquires GPS. CamCompanion pushes your phone's time to the camera when you connect, which corrects future recordings.
Does CamCompanion collect my data?
No. Everything stays on your device and your camera. See the Privacy Policy.
How do I report a problem with my specific camera?
In Settings → Diagnostics, turn on Diagnostics, reproduce the issue, then share the diagnostic report with me. Wi-Fi passwords are removed automatically.
